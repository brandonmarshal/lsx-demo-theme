### What have you learned?

-   Discovered heavy limitations with Figma's AI agent, specifically its lack of contextual logic for basic layout tuning (like adding padding). It requires intense "spoon-feeding," and it is often faster to fix components manually in a separate file.
-   Gained a deeper understanding of the complexity of Figma prototype routing and underlying logic, particularly when encountering severe state issues like the black screen rendering on the Tour Operator prototype.
-   Realized the necessity of fallback debugging techniques: when in-platform AI or basic audits fail on complex errors (like LS publishing or WooCommerce GitHub commits), exporting the codebase to an IDE for review by more capable coding models is a highly effective next step.
